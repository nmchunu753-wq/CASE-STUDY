#☕ Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

📊 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of Bright Coffee Shop, aimed at understanding business performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends across different locations, time periods, and product categories.

The goal of this analysis is to uncover key insights and provide data-driven recommendations to improve sales, customer experience, and overall business growth.
🏪 About the Business

Bright Coffee Shop is a cozy, premium café that focuses on:

Delivering high-quality coffee at affordable prices
Using fresh, locally sourced ingredients
Creating a warm and welcoming environment
Offering a mix of classic and innovative products
🎯 Objectives
Analyze sales performance across different months
Identify best and worst selling products
Understand customer purchasing behavior by day and time
Compare revenue across locations
Provide actionable business recommendations
📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

-Transaction dates and times
-Product categories (Coffee, Tea, Bakery, etc.)


-Store locations:
Hell’s Kitchen
Astoria
Lower Manhattan

📈 Key Findings
💰 Overall Sales
Total sales (Jan–Jun): R698,812.33
Sales increased significantly during colder months (May–June)
📅 Monthly Trends
January–February: Lowest sales (summer period)
March–April: Gradual increase (rainfall influence)
May–June: Peak sales (winter season)
🛍️ Product Performance

Top-selling products:

Coffee
Tea
Bakery items
Drinking chocolate
Flavoured coffee beans

Worst-selling products:

Branded products
Loose tea
Packaged chocolate

👉 Insight:

Lower-priced items sell more (high turnover)
Expensive products perform poorly (low visibility or value perception)
🕒 Time-Based Insights
Mid-mornings have the highest sales
Customers buying before/going to work
Cold weather increases demand


📆 Day-Based Insights

Monday: Highest revenue (commuter traffic)
Saturday: Lowest revenue (different routines)
📍 Location Analysis
Hell’s Kitchen: Highest revenue (high foot traffic)
Lower Manhattan: Lowest revenue (high competition)
💡 Recommendations
📉 Improve Low-Selling Products
Adjust pricing strategies
Improve product visibility and promotion
Use social media marketing and influencers
Gather customer feedback (polls/surveys)
🌞 Boost Summer Sales (Jan–Feb)

Trending seasonal products:

Iced coffee
Ice tea
Coffee-flavoured ice cream
🎉 Customer Engagement Strategies
Host events (coffee tastings, latte art classes)
Introduce loyalty programs (e.g., Buy 1 Get 1 Free)
Expand menu:
Light meals (salads, avocado toast)
Vegan and gluten-free options

This project highlights key factors affecting Bright Coffee Shop’s performance and provides actionable insights to drive growth. By leveraging these recommendations, the business can:
